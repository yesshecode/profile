About me

I was born and raised in Brazil but have been calling Canada home since 2013. I have a bachelors degree in business and am a certified professional project manager (PMI) since 2011.

I have managed projects accross different industries since the beginning of my career in 2006. It was a long journey… I worked in research & development, mining, oil & gas, then I landed a job in IT as a project manager for web/software development. I finally found home! Here is where my passion for web development begins.

While managing projects I learnt how to design and build beautiful and responsive websites and have been building custom websites for clients around the world, in both english and portuguese.

I love dogs, but don’t have one. I learnt english as an adult and have been diving deep into self-development. If you want to know more about me, check my blog or let’s have a coffee!
